# Mental Health Phrase Classifier

# Problem Statement
- ManualAnalysis: Identifying mental health states from text is time-consuming and subjective.  
- NeedForAutomation: Users express emotions in chats or journals that are difficult to analyze at scale.  
- InsightDelay: Lack of real-time tools to provide timely mental health insights.  

# Scope of the Project
- TextClassification: Classify short text phrases into mental health categories.  
- RealTime: Provide quick predictions with confidence scores.  
- FallbackRules: Handle ambiguous or low-confidence inputs for robust results.  
- Scalable: Can be expanded with larger datasets and advanced NLP models.  

# Target Users
- Students: Track stress, anxiety, or motivation levels in daily life.  
- Professionals: Monitor workplace mental health through text inputs.  
- MentalHealthPractitioners: Assist in analyzing textual expressions of mental state.  
- Researchers: Study patterns in mental health phrases at scale.  

# High-Level Features
- UserInput: Accept phrases via command-line interface (CLI).  
- Prediction: Categorize input as stress, anxiety, motivation, or neutral.  
- ConfidenceScore: Display prediction confidence.  
- ModelPersistence: Save/load trained model and vectorizer for efficiency.  
- FallbackRules: Apply keyword-based rules for uncertain predictions.
